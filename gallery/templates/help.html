Please note, you may not be able to download Watermarked images.

Download Images:
   Computer or Laptop:

      - Visit your gallery.
      - Open an image by clicking on it.
      - Click the Download icon ( ) or Right-Click and press Save-As.


   iOS Devices (iPhone, iPad):

      - Visit your gallery.
      - Open an image by tapping on it.
      - Click the Download icon ( ).
      - Press View.
      - Hold down on the image and press Add to Photos.


   Android Devices (Samsung, Google, etc.):

      - Visit your gallery.
      - Open an image by tapping on it.
      - Click the Download icon ( ) or Hold Down on the image and press Download Image.


Download Videos:
   Computer or Laptop:

      - Visit your video gallery.
      - Click a video link.
      - Right Click the video and press Save Video As.


   iOS Devices - Safari (iPhone, iPad):

      - Visit your video gallery.
      - Hold Down on a video link.
      - Press Download Linked File to Download it.
      - Press the Downlod Icon ( in a circle ) of Safari (in the top-right corner).
      - Press on the downloaded video to open it.
      - Press the Share Icon ( ) in the bottom-left corner.
      - Choose Save Video to save it to your camera roll or any other option to send it to someone.


   Android Devices (Samsung, Google, etc.):

      - Visit your video gallery
      - Press a video link to open it.
      - Click the Settings icon ( ) in the bottom right.
      - Press the Download button.


If you are still having trouble downloading images or video, please contact us.
